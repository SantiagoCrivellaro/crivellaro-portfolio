Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions about working with me.

How long does a typical project take?
Most projects take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. A simple chatbot or single automation can be done in under 2 weeks. Multi-step systems with custom AI models take 4-6 weeks. You'll get a clear timeline in the proposal.
What if I need changes after the project is done?
Every project includes at least one round of revisions built into the price. After launch, you get a support period (duration depends on the plan) for bug fixes and tweaks. For ongoing changes, the Retainer plan is the best option.
Do I need technical knowledge to use the solutions you build?
No. Everything I build comes with documentation and a hands-on training session. The goal is for your team to operate the system independently. If something breaks, I'm a message away during the support period.
What tools and platforms do you work with?
I work with OpenAI (GPT-4), Claude, LangChain, Python, n8n, Make.com, Zapier, FastAPI, and most popular APIs (Slack, Notion, Airtable, Shopify, HubSpot, etc). If your stack isn't listed, just ask — I adapt quickly.
How much does it cost?
Projects start at $500 for simple automations and go up to $5,000+ for enterprise solutions. Check the pricing section for details, or book a free discovery call and I'll give you an exact quote based on your needs.
Can you work with my existing systems?
Absolutely. Integration is a core part of what I do. I connect AI solutions to your existing tools — CRMs, databases, communication platforms, document management systems — via APIs and webhooks.
Do you offer ongoing maintenance?
Yes. The Retainer plan gives you 20 hours/month of development, priority support, and proactive optimization. It's ideal for businesses that want continuous improvement of their AI systems.
